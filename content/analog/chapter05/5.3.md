## Circuits & Formulas

### Switch

$$
i_B = \frac{v_I-V_{BE}({\rm on})}{R_B}
$$

$$
i_C = \frac{V_{CC}-V_{CE}({\rm sat})}{R_C}
$$

$$
v_O = V_{CE}({\rm sat})
$$

These equations are true when the diode is in saturation. If $v_I = V_{CC}$ and $V_{BE}({\rm on}) \approx V_{CE}({\rm sat})$, then $R_B/R_C \leq \beta$.
